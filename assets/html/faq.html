Frequently Asked Questions

Can I scale resources automatically?

Yes, our platform automatically scales resources based on traffic, CPU usage, and performance metrics for Starter plans and above.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! You can start a 7-day free trial to explore basic cloud deployment features before choosing a paid plan.

Do you provide data backup and recovery?

Yes, we offer automated backups and rapid recovery solutions to ensure your business data is always protected and easily restorable.

What type of cloud infrastructure do you use?

Our platform is built on modern, distributed cloud infrastructure designed for high availability, performance, and reliability across multiple regions.

Can teams collaborate on the platform?

Absolutely. Cloud Application supports team collaboration with role-based access control, allowing organizations to manage permissions securely and efficiently.

Which integrations are included in each plan?

Starter includes Slack for basic team messaging. Pro adds Docker and GitHub for containerized deployments and version control. Advanced includes Kubernetes and monitoring tools. Enterprise provides all integrations plus Google Cloud, premium analytics, and enhanced security features.

Can I monitor my application’s performance in real-time?

Yes! Our platform provides real-time dashboards to monitor CPU, memory, traffic, and error rates so you can optimize your apps efficiently.

Does the platform support multi-region deployment?

Absolutely. You can deploy your applications across multiple regions to reduce latency, ensure redundancy, and provide a seamless experience for global users.
